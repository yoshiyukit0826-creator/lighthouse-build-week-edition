# Demo Video Plan — Under 3 Minutes

## Recommended format

- Japanese narration by the operator
- English subtitles burned into the video
- Public YouTube upload
- Target duration: 2:35–2:50

## Timeline

### 0:00–0:20 — Problem

Show the title and desktop overview. Explain that unfinished work, preparation, unexpected events, and limited capacity can narrow a person's view before the next decision.

### 0:20–0:45 — Morning Check

Show R/S/Y on mobile. Explain that R is remaining work, S is preparation, and Y is available margin. The values establish the initial state rather than scoring the person.

### 0:45–1:15 — HP and SP

Show the HP ring and action controls. Trigger one recovery, one decrease, and one preparation action. Explain HP as current capability and SP as future reserve.

### 1:15–1:45 — Adaptive Beacon

Show current position, next step, and alternate route. Explain that Lighthouse presents options rather than one mandatory command.

### 1:45–2:15 — Trajectory Review and JUNCTION

Open LOG PAUSE / LAST 8, select a turning point, and choose Continue, Detour, or Decide Later. Explain that the choice influences the next guidance without rewriting the past.

### 2:15–2:40 — GPT-5.6 / ARK and Build Week

Explain that GPT-5.6, called ARK in the project, worked as a co-designer: structuring field experience, checking contradictions, refining interaction design, and preserving context. Clearly separate the pre-existing Sheets core from the responsive Web HUD and JUNCTION work added for Build Week.

### 2:40–2:50 — Closing

End on the lighthouse and the line:

> A navigation system that grows with its operator.

> It does not manage people. It reflects where they are and illuminates the next step and another route.
